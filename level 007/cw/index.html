cristiano ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro GOIH ComM (born 5 February 1985) is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward for Saudi Pro League club Al Nassr and captains the Portugal national team. Often considered the best player in the world and widely regarded as one of the greatest players of all time, Ronaldo has won five Ballon d'Or awards, a record for a European player, and four European Golden Shoes. He has won 32 major trophies in his career, including seven league titles, five UEFA Champions League titles, one UEFA European Championship, and one UEFA Nations League title. He is also the first player to win league titles in England, Spain, and Italy.

Ronaldo began his senior club career playing for Sporting CP, before signing with Manchester

United in 2003, at age 18, for a transfer fee of £12.24 million, making him the most expensive teenager in English football history. He won three Premier League titles, one FA Cup, two Football League Cups, and the UEFA Champions League during his six seasons with the club. In 2009, Ronaldo moved to Real Madrid for a then-world record transfer fee of £80 million. He won two La Liga titles, two Copas del Rey, four UEFA Champions League titles, and three FIFA Club World Cups with Madrid. In 2018, he transferred to Juventus for €100 million, winning two Serie A titles and one Coppa Italia over three seasons. In December 2022, Ronaldo signed with Al Nassr.
